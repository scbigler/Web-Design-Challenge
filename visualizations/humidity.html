vs. Humidity

This chart indicates that high humidity happens at nearly all latitiudes in the dataset. However, at 0 degrees latitude (the equator) there seems to be higher humidity than low humidity in cities represented in the dataset. In other words, there are much fewer low or no low humidity days near the equator in relation to other latitudes. This is true for both southern and nothern latitides.
